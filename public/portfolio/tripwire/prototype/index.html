Items Identified

Physical items containing confidential and/or proprietary information should be secured in a locked cabinet, drawer or office during extended periods away from workspace such as lunch break.

Screen-lock, log off or shut down computers and printers when unattended for extended periods. Password-protected screen savers should be set to automatically activate within 15 minutes or less of user inactivity.

Trash of a sensitive nature should always be shredded using the office facilities. Ensure items are removed from meeting areas post meeting.

Lock or otherwise secure portable computer devices when away from area for extended periods of time.

Refrain from cluttering the work area with Post-It® or other handwritten notes displaying sensitive information such as user ID’s, passwords, account numbers, etc.
